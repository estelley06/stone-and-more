About Us

Lebanon's Trusted Stone Specialists

Stone & More has been Lebanon's trusted supplier of premium building and finishing materials for over two decades. From the mountains of Zgharta-Miziara to the coast of Batroun-Madfoun, we bring the finest products to homes and projects across the country.

Our team works directly with clients, architects, and contractors to help select the perfect materials — combining authentic craftsmanship with modern design sensibility.
